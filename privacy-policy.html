2. What Data We Collect

This data is used to process your service request and ensure seamless customer experience.

b) Website and Subscription Data

If you subscribe to services via our website or email, we may collect:

  • Email address

  • Banking details (secure)
  • Vehicle details (for product installation and recovery services)

We use this data only to manage your subscription or provide relevant updates.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To identify you as a user (via your User ID)

4. When We Share Data

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing.

Your data may be shared only when:

  • Required by law or legal obligation

5. Location Data

We access your GPS location only when you press a button in the app that requires it (e.g., for reporting or assistance). Location tracking does not run in the background.

Your data is securely stored and processed in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) and applicable international standards. We take all reasonable measures to protect your data from loss, misuse, or unauthorized access.

9. Data Deletion Request – StaySecure 365°

The StaySecure 365° mobile application developed by Mollorobotics (Pty) Ltd allows users to submit contact information (such as name, phone number, and optional vehicle information) when activating the panic alert feature.

This information is transmitted to our control room for emergency response purposes. Users may optionally choose to save certain information on their device within the application to support the functionality of the StaySecure 365° service.

Users may request deletion of any personal information associated with their use of the StaySecure 365° service.

How to Request Deletion

  • Visit our contact page: https://mollorobotics.co.za/contact-us/
  • Select “Account / Data Deletion Request” from the subject dropdown.
  • Provide one of the following so we can identify your request:
    • Phone number used when activating the alert
    • Email address associated with the service
    • Order number (if applicable)
  • Once verified, the request will be processed within 7 business days.
